Privacy Policy

For Paperly Notes / Notebook App

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Paperly Notes, also referred to as the Notebook App, handles your information when you use the app.

Paperly Notes is designed as a simple notes and notebook app. Your notes are stored locally on your own device/browser unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal information.

The app may store the following information locally in your browser:

  • Note titles
  • Note content
  • Categories
  • Favorite or pinned note status
  • Theme preference such as light mode or dark mode
  • Created and updated timestamps

2. Local Storage

Paperly Notes uses your browser's localStorage to save your notes and preferences. This means your data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

If you clear your browser data, reset your browser, or use another device, your saved notes may no longer be available unless you have exported a backup.

3. No Account Required

Paperly Notes does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal information to use the app.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

This app does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics tools. We do not monitor your notes or usage activity.

5. Import and Export Features

The app may allow you to export notes as TXT files or export/import a JSON backup. These files are controlled by you. Please keep your exported files safe because they may contain your personal notes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your notes or personal data with third parties. Your notes remain in your browser unless you personally export, copy, or share them.

7. Data Security

We use a local-first approach to help keep your notes private. However, because your data is stored in your browser, you are responsible for keeping your device, browser, and exported backup files secure.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your notes directly inside the app. You can also remove all saved data by clearing your browser's localStorage or site data for this app.

9. Children's Privacy

Paperly Notes is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect data on a server, any notes created remain locally on the user's device/browser.

10. Third-Party Services

Paperly Notes does not depend on third-party services to store your notes. If the app is hosted on a platform such as GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel, that hosting provider may process basic technical information according to its own policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
